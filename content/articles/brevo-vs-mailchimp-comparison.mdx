## Brevo vs Mailchimp: The Complete Comparison

Mailchimp is the world's most recognized email marketing brand. It pioneered the freemium email marketing model and built a massive user base on the strength of its early simplicity and generous free plan. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) took a different approach: unlimited contacts on every plan, volume-based pricing, and a broader set of tools including SMS, live chat, CRM, and transactional email — all in one platform.

Choosing between Brevo and Mailchimp is one of the most common decisions email marketers face. This guide compares them honestly across every dimension that matters.

## Pricing: The Biggest Difference

Pricing is where Brevo and Mailchimp diverge most dramatically — and where the comparison often decisively favors Brevo for most businesses.

### Mailchimp's Pricing Model

Mailchimp charges based on the number of contacts on your list. As your list grows, your monthly cost automatically increases — even if you are not sending more emails.

Mailchimp's pricing tiers (approximate, as of 2024):
- **Free:** Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month
- **Essentials:** From $13/month (500 contacts) to $350+/month (50,000 contacts)
- **Standard:** From $20/month (500 contacts) to $545+/month (50,000 contacts)
- **Premium:** From $350/month (10,000 contacts) up

The jump in cost as your list grows is steep. A business with 25,000 contacts on Mailchimp Standard pays around $270/month. The same business on Mailchimp Premium might pay $500+/month.

### Brevo's Pricing Model

Brevo charges based on email sending volume, not contact count. Your list can be unlimited — you never pay more just because your list grows.

- **Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts
- **Starter:** From $25/month for 20,000 emails
- **Business:** From $65/month for 20,000 emails
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for high-volume senders

A business with 25,000 contacts sending 2 campaigns per month (50,000 total emails) on Brevo Business pays approximately $65-80/month — compared to $270-545/month on comparable Mailchimp plans.

**Verdict:** For businesses with growing lists, Brevo is significantly less expensive than Mailchimp. The savings compound as your list grows.

## Email Design and Templates

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp's drag-and-drop email builder is polished and intuitive. It includes over 100 pre-designed templates organized by use case and industry. The builder supports responsive design with a strong mobile preview tool.

Mailchimp's Creative Assistant (AI-powered design tool) can generate branded templates from your website's colors and logo — a genuinely useful feature for quick campaign creation.

### Brevo

Brevo's drag-and-drop editor is comparable to Mailchimp's in functionality. It is slightly less polished aesthetically but offers the same core capabilities: blocks, templates, responsive preview, and HTML editing.

Brevo's template library is smaller than Mailchimp's but covers all major use cases. The platform also supports custom HTML templates for full design control.

**Verdict:** Mailchimp has a slight edge in design polish and template variety. Brevo's editor is fully capable but lacks some of Mailchimp's design finesse.

## Marketing Automation

### Mailchimp Automation

Mailchimp's automation is solid but has become fragmented across plan tiers. The free plan includes basic automations. The Standard plan unlocks multi-step automation journeys. The Premium plan adds more advanced triggers and conditional branches.

Mailchimp's Customer Journey Builder (Standard plan) provides a visual workflow editor that is genuinely powerful, though some advanced behavioral triggers are limited compared to dedicated automation platforms.

### Brevo Automation

Brevo's automation is available on all plans (with more features on Business and Enterprise) and supports a wider range of trigger types, including website tracking triggers that track page visits. The visual workflow builder is intuitive and allows complex multi-branch conditional logic.

Key advantages of Brevo automation:
- SMS and email actions in the same workflow
- Webhook actions for connecting to external systems
- Contact attribute update actions
- API-triggered workflows

**Verdict:** Brevo offers more flexible and comprehensive automation, especially for multi-channel (email + SMS) workflows and API-driven automation.

## Contact Management and Segmentation

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp uses an audience-based model where contacts belong to specific audiences. Contacts in multiple audiences are counted separately and billed as separate contacts — a significant cost issue for businesses with complex list structures.

Segmentation in Mailchimp is powerful, with support for demographic, behavioral, and tag-based segments. Mailchimp's predictive segmentation (Premium plan) uses machine learning to identify contacts likely to purchase or churn.

### Brevo

Brevo uses a unified contact model: one contact exists once, regardless of how many lists they belong to. This prevents duplicate billing and simplifies management.

Brevo's segmentation is flexible with attribute-based and behavioral conditions. Segments update dynamically in real time as contact data changes.

**Verdict:** Brevo's contact model is more logical and cost-efficient. Mailchimp's multi-audience model creates complexity and inflates costs for businesses with overlapping lists.

## Multi-Channel Marketing

### Mailchimp

Mailchimp added SMS marketing (Mailchimp SMS) but it is limited and only available in the US. Mailchimp also offers landing pages, social media ads integration, and postcard sending (US only). The platform leans primarily toward email marketing.

### Brevo

Brevo is genuinely multi-channel:
- Email marketing
- SMS marketing (global)
- WhatsApp messaging (selected markets)
- Push notifications
- Live chat
- CRM
- Transactional email (SMTP + API)
- Landing pages

All channels are integrated in the same platform, using the same contact database and the same automation workflows.

**Verdict:** Brevo is a true multi-channel platform. Mailchimp is primarily an email marketing tool with limited channel expansion.

## Deliverability

Both Brevo and Mailchimp maintain strong deliverability reputations. Brevo's deliverability is generally rated slightly higher in independent tests, partly due to stricter anti-spam policies and a more rigorous sender verification process.

Both platforms offer domain authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), dedicated IPs (Enterprise), and feedback loop integration with major email providers.

**Verdict:** Roughly equivalent, with Brevo having a slight edge in independent benchmarks.

## Ease of Use

Mailchimp has a significant advantage in brand familiarity and onboarding experience. New users find Mailchimp's interface more intuitive, and the platform has more extensive documentation, tutorials, and community resources.

Brevo has a steeper initial learning curve but becomes highly efficient once users are familiar with the platform. The breadth of features (CRM, SMS, chat, transactional email) can feel overwhelming at first.

**Verdict:** Mailchimp is easier for absolute beginners. Brevo rewards the learning investment with more capability.

## Who Should Choose Brevo

- Businesses with lists of 2,000+ contacts who want to avoid contact-based pricing
- Multi-channel marketers who need email + SMS in one platform
- Businesses needing transactional email alongside marketing email
- E-commerce businesses who need deep automation at a reasonable cost
- Budget-conscious marketers who need more for less

## Who Should Choose Mailchimp

- Absolute beginners who prioritize simplicity over breadth
- Businesses with very small lists (under 500 contacts) where Mailchimp's free plan is sufficient
- Marketers who prioritize template design and Mailchimp's Creative Assistant
- Businesses already deeply integrated into Mailchimp's ecosystem
