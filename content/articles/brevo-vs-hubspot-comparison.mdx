# Brevo vs HubSpot: Email Marketing Platform Compared

HubSpot is the dominant all-in-one marketing platform for mid-sized to enterprise businesses. Brevo is a powerful, affordable alternative that delivers a surprising amount of the same functionality — email marketing, CRM, automation, SMS — at a fraction of the cost. This comparison helps you understand when HubSpot's premium is justified and when Brevo is the smarter choice.

## The Fundamental Difference

**HubSpot** is built around its CRM as the foundation, with marketing, sales, and service hubs layered on top. It is deeply powerful but carries a price tag that reflects enterprise positioning. The free CRM is genuinely useful, but meaningful marketing functionality requires paid Marketing Hub plans that start at $800/month for just 2,000 contacts.

**Brevo** is built around email and SMS delivery, with a CRM included as part of the package rather than as a separate, upsell-heavy hub. The value proposition is straightforward: send more email for less money, with capable automation and CRM features included.

## Pricing Comparison: Brevo vs HubSpot

**HubSpot Free CRM:** Unlimited contacts, basic email (2,000/month limit, HubSpot branding), basic deal tracking. Functional but limited.

**HubSpot Marketing Hub Starter:** From $20/month — 1,000 contacts, 10x contact limit emails/month. Price scales quickly with contacts.

**HubSpot Marketing Hub Professional:** From $800/month — required for automation, A/B testing, custom reporting, and most advanced features. Minimum 2,000 contacts.

**HubSpot Marketing Hub Enterprise:** From $3,600/month.

**Brevo Free:** 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts, full CRM, basic automation.
**Brevo Starter:** From $9/month, 5,000 emails/month, unlimited contacts.
**Brevo Business:** From $18/month, 20,000 emails/month, full automation, A/B testing.
**Brevo Enterprise:** Custom pricing.

**Verdict on pricing:** There is no comparison for small to mid-sized businesses. The features that HubSpot requires an $800/month plan to access (marketing automation, A/B testing, advanced reporting) are available in Brevo's Business plan at $18/month.

## Email Marketing Features

**Brevo** offers a complete email marketing suite: drag-and-drop builder, template library, segmentation, personalisation, A/B testing (on Business), send time optimisation, and spam check.

**HubSpot** offers similar email marketing capabilities, but with deeper personalisation powered by its CRM data. Because HubSpot's CRM is more mature and deeply integrated, email personalisation using deal stage, lifecycle stage, and custom CRM properties is more seamless.

**Winner: Brevo** on value. **HubSpot** on depth of CRM-driven personalisation for complex customer journeys.

## CRM Capabilities

**HubSpot CRM** is genuinely best-in-class for its tier. Contact and company records are rich. Deal pipelines are flexible. The activity timeline (calls, emails, meetings, notes) is comprehensive. Integration with Gmail and Outlook for two-way email sync is seamless. For sales-heavy businesses managing complex B2B deals, HubSpot's CRM is hard to replace.

**Brevo CRM** is functional and sufficient for most small business needs. You can manage contacts, track deals through a pipeline, assign tasks, and view email engagement history. It is not as feature-rich as HubSpot's CRM, but it covers the basics at no additional cost.

**Winner: HubSpot** for complex B2B sales processes. **Brevo** for businesses that need basic CRM without paying for an enterprise platform.

## Marketing Automation

**HubSpot's workflow automation** (Professional plan and above, $800+/month) is extremely powerful. Workflows can branch across the entire HubSpot ecosystem — updating CRM records, enrolling contacts in sequences, creating tasks for sales reps, triggering SMS through integrations, and connecting to hundreds of native integrations.

**Brevo's automation** is available from the Business plan ($18/month). Workflows support email, SMS, CRM updates, list management, and conditional branching. For most businesses, Brevo's automation is more than capable of handling lead nurturing, welcome sequences, and behavioural triggers.

**Winner: HubSpot** for enterprise-grade, multi-tool automation. **Brevo** for businesses that need capable automation at an accessible price.

## Reporting and Analytics

**HubSpot** provides some of the most sophisticated marketing analytics available, including attribution reporting (first touch, last touch, linear, time decay), custom dashboards, revenue reporting, and campaign influence tracking.

**Brevo** provides solid campaign-level analytics: open rates, click rates, heatmaps, geographic data, device breakdown, and basic conversion tracking. Advanced attribution across multiple campaigns and channels requires external tools.

**Winner: HubSpot** for comprehensive multi-touch attribution and revenue reporting. **Brevo** for standard email campaign analytics.

## Integrations

HubSpot's integration ecosystem is one of the most extensive in the marketing technology industry — over 1,400 native integrations. Virtually every business tool has a HubSpot integration.

Brevo's integration library is smaller but covers the major platforms. For businesses running on standard tech stacks (Shopify, WordPress, Salesforce, Zapier), Brevo's integrations are sufficient.

**Winner: HubSpot** for breadth of integrations.

## Ease of Use

HubSpot's interface has improved significantly over the years but remains complex for new users — primarily because the platform does so much that the learning curve is substantial.

Brevo is notably simpler to get started with. Most users can send their first campaign within an hour of signing up without requiring training.

**Winner: Brevo** for ease of getting started.

## Who Should Choose Brevo?

- Small to mid-sized businesses that need professional email marketing + basic CRM without enterprise pricing
- Teams where email marketing is the primary channel
- Businesses with large contact lists where HubSpot's contact-based pricing becomes prohibitive
- Startups and growing businesses that need cost-effective automation
- Any business where the $800/month HubSpot Professional plan is not justified by revenue

## Who Should Choose HubSpot?

- Mid-to-large B2B companies with complex sales cycles and multiple deal stages
- Teams that need deep integration between marketing, sales, and service functions
- Businesses where two-way email sync between reps' Gmail/Outlook and a CRM is critical
- Companies that need enterprise-grade attribution reporting across multiple marketing channels
- Organizations already using HubSpot's Sales or Service Hub
