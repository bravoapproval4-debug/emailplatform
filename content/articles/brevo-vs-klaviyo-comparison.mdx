## Brevo vs Klaviyo: Two Platforms, Two Philosophies

Klaviyo is the dominant email marketing platform for direct-to-consumer (DTC) brands and e-commerce businesses. Built specifically for commerce, it offers deep Shopify integration, predictive analytics, and powerful behavioral segmentation based on purchase history.

Brevo is a broader marketing platform that serves businesses of all types — from B2B SaaS companies to small retail businesses to enterprise organizations. It competes on price, channel breadth, and overall accessibility.

Choosing between Brevo and Klaviyo depends heavily on your business model, your tech stack, and what you need email marketing to accomplish.

## Pricing Comparison

Pricing is the most dramatic difference between these two platforms — Klaviyo is significantly more expensive than Brevo, especially as your list grows.

### Klaviyo Pricing

Klaviyo uses contact-based pricing:
- **Free:** Up to 250 contacts, 500 emails/month
- **Email:** From $20/month for 500 contacts to $1,700+/month for 150,000 contacts
- **Email + SMS:** Add 15% to email pricing for SMS inclusion

For a business with 50,000 contacts on Klaviyo Email, the monthly cost is approximately $700/month. For 100,000 contacts, it exceeds $1,400/month.

### Brevo Pricing

Brevo uses volume-based pricing with unlimited contacts:
- **Free:** $0/month, 300 emails/day, unlimited contacts
- **Starter:** From $25/month for 20,000 emails
- **Business:** From $65/month for 20,000 emails
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

A business with 50,000 contacts sending 4 campaigns/month (200,000 emails) on Brevo Business pays approximately $150-250/month — versus ~$700/month on Klaviyo.

**Verdict:** Brevo is dramatically less expensive for large lists. For very small lists (under 500 contacts), Klaviyo's free plan offers more capability than Brevo's free plan.

## E-Commerce Integration

### Klaviyo

Klaviyo was purpose-built for e-commerce. Its Shopify integration is considered the best in the industry:

- Syncs full customer purchase history in real time
- Tracks website behavior (product viewed, category browsed, search terms)
- Powers flows like abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase, and win-back
- Enables predictive analytics: predicted next purchase date, expected lifetime value, churn risk
- Segments contacts by product categories purchased, average order value, or SKU-level data

If your business is heavily e-commerce with Shopify as the foundation, Klaviyo's depth of commerce data integration is genuinely class-leading.

### Brevo

Brevo integrates with Shopify, WooCommerce, PrestaShop, and Magento. It supports basic e-commerce tracking: orders synced to contact records, abandoned cart recovery, and post-purchase sequences.

However, Brevo's commerce integration is less deep than Klaviyo's. Predictive analytics (predicted CLV, churn probability) are not native features. Product recommendation blocks and real-time inventory-based email triggers are less developed.

**Verdict:** Klaviyo wins decisively for deep e-commerce integration, especially with Shopify. Brevo's integration is sufficient for basic e-commerce email but cannot match Klaviyo's sophistication.

## Segmentation and Personalization

### Klaviyo

Klaviyo's segmentation is extraordinarily powerful for commerce:
- Segment by product viewed, category browsed, number of orders
- Predictive segments: "likely to purchase in next 7 days," "at risk of churning"
- Real-time segments that update based on website behavior seconds after it happens

### Brevo

Brevo's segmentation uses contact attributes and email behavior. It can incorporate data from integrations, but does not have the native predictive analytics layer that Klaviyo offers.

However, for non-e-commerce use cases (SaaS, B2B, service businesses), Brevo's segmentation is typically sufficient — the additional depth of Klaviyo is specifically designed around purchase behavior, which is irrelevant without a substantial commerce data set.

**Verdict:** Klaviyo wins for commerce-native segmentation. Brevo is fully capable for non-commerce segmentation needs.

## Automation and Flows

### Klaviyo "Flows"

Klaviyo calls its automation "Flows." These are powerful for e-commerce:
- Pre-built flows for abandoned cart, welcome series, browse abandonment, post-purchase, and sunset
- AI-powered flow optimization that suggests changes based on performance data
- SMS and email in the same flow
- Deep product personalization using real-time catalog data

### Brevo Workflows

Brevo's automation workflows are flexible and capable for a broad range of use cases. The workflow builder supports multi-branch conditional logic, email and SMS actions, contact attribute updates, and webhook triggers.

Where Brevo's automation is weaker than Klaviyo is in commerce-specific triggers: Klaviyo can trigger flows on "viewed specific product" or "added to cart but did not purchase" with more precision than Brevo's current integration allows.

**Verdict:** Klaviyo's flows are superior for e-commerce automation. Brevo's workflows are more flexible for non-commerce automation.

## Reporting and Analytics

### Klaviyo

Klaviyo's reporting is commerce-centric and excellent:
- Revenue attributed per email and per flow
- Benchmark data comparing your metrics to industry averages
- Predictive CLV and churn dashboards
- Detailed attribution windows configurable per campaign

### Brevo

Brevo's reporting covers standard email marketing metrics: opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, and per-link click data. Revenue attribution requires custom UTM setup and external analytics.

**Verdict:** Klaviyo wins for revenue-focused e-commerce reporting. Brevo's reporting is adequate for standard email metrics.

## Non-E-Commerce Capabilities

Here, the comparison shifts in Brevo's favor:

**Brevo offers:**
- Full SMS marketing (global, not just US)
- Live chat software
- Built-in CRM for sales pipeline management
- Transactional email (SMTP and API)
- WhatsApp marketing (selected markets)
- Landing page builder

**Klaviyo offers:**
- Email marketing (deep e-commerce focus)
- SMS (primarily US)
- Mobile push notifications
- Product reviews (Klaviyo Reviews)

For businesses that need email + CRM + live chat + SMS + transactional email in a single platform, Brevo is the clear winner. Klaviyo is built for commerce, not for broad multi-channel marketing.

## Who Should Choose Brevo

- B2B businesses, SaaS companies, service businesses
- Businesses with large lists (10,000+ contacts) that cannot afford Klaviyo's pricing
- Businesses needing multi-channel: email + SMS + CRM + live chat
- Early-stage e-commerce businesses where Klaviyo's cost is prohibitive
- Non-Shopify e-commerce businesses where Klaviyo's advantages are reduced

## Who Should Choose Klaviyo

- Shopify-based DTC brands with high transaction volumes
- E-commerce businesses where predictive analytics drive strategy
- Brands willing to pay a premium for best-in-class e-commerce email
- Businesses where Klaviyo's revenue attribution is a strategic requirement
